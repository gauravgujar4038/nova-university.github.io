About Nova University

Excellence in education since 1998

Who We Are

Nova University is a leading institution committed to nurturing innovation, critical thinking, and global leadership. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values required to excel in a rapidly evolving world. We combine world-class faculty with experiential learning opportunities to shape future innovators and leaders.

Our Journey

Founded in 1998, Nova University has grown from a small local college into a globally recognized university. We have expanded our academic programs, research initiatives, and partnerships with top universities around the world. Our alumni network is spread across continents, contributing to society in business, technology, education, and research.

Achievements

  • Ranked among the top 10 universities nationally for academic excellence.
  • Over 500 research publications annually with international collaborations.
  • Alumni network across 30+ countries holding leadership positions.
  • Recognized for innovation in STEM, business, and humanities programs.
  • Strong industry partnerships providing students with internships and practical experience.
